About Us

Specializing in Fiber Optics, Serving Extreme Environments

Guangbo Optoelectronics Co., Ltd.

We specialize in fiber optic cable R&D and manufacturing, providing professional signal transmission solutions for UAVs and USVs in extreme electromagnetic environments.

Guangbo Optoelectronics Co., Ltd. was established in August 2024 as a high-tech enterprise specializing in fiber optic cable R&D, production, and sales. The company is located in Shacheng Industrial Park, Chaisang District, Jiujiang City, Jiangxi Province, with a production base area of 2,000 square meters.

Since its establishment, the company has always adhered to the development philosophy of technological innovation and quality priority. In September 2024, the company established a cooperative relationship with Jiangxi Xunzhun Intelligent Technology Co., Ltd., beginning the sales business of fiber optic gyroscopes and accumulating rich industry experience. In November 2024, the company entered into strategic cooperation with Nanchang Hangkong University to jointly develop fiber optic cable products with inner winding technology, further enhancing technical strength.

Currently, the company's main products include two series: fiber optic cables and fiber optic rewinding machines, widely used in UAV communication, USV control, underground pipeline detection, special operations, and other fields. The products cover various specifications from 1km to 50km, featuring G.657A2 fiber with ultra-fine diameter, ultra-low loss, and excellent bend resistance.
